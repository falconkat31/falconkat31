Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together.

What do you do as a Book Marketing Specialist?

I develop and execute comprehensive marketing strategies for books including Amazon optimization, social media campaigns, email marketing, review generation, and author branding—all customized to your goals.

How do I get started?

Simply email me at falconkat31@gmail.com with your book information and goals. We'll discuss via email, I'll audit your situation, develop a custom strategy, and present a detailed proposal.

Why only email communication?

Email ensures complete accountability and documentation. Every conversation, decision, and result is tracked, creating a permanent record of our partnership. It's professional and allows thoughtful responses.

What results can I expect?

Results vary by genre, competition, and strategy. Common outcomes include improved rankings, increased reviews, higher sales, and enhanced visibility. I'll set realistic expectations during our consultation.

How does pricing work?

Every author's needs are different, so I develop custom proposals based on your goals, timeline, and required services. Investment details are discussed via email after I understand your project.

How long before I see results?

Some results like ranking improvements can happen within days. Sales momentum typically builds over 4-8 weeks. Platform growth is longer-term. We'll map specific milestones together.
